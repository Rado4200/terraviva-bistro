Our Story

Passion on Every Plate

At TerraViva Bistro, we bring the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean coast to the heart of Barcelona. Our chef, Maria Oliveira, combines traditional recipes with modern techniques to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Every ingredient is carefully sourced from local markets and sustainable farms, ensuring that each dish tells a story of quality, freshness, and respect for our beautiful Mediterranean heritage.
